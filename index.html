Who We Are

The Pure Arabian Horses culture. Arabs in Syria consider horses as their companions on the long journey from past to present. This precious part of our culture has shaped the nature and personalities of Syrians.

Al Jadaan Stables of Arabian Horses was established in Damascus. With many public and private stables, Al Jadaan Stables has done its utmost to protect the pure Arabian Syrian Horse and to promote its importance in local and international fields. Furthermore Al Jadaan Stables has taken steps to present the Syrian Arabian Horses as a unique group worldwide.
